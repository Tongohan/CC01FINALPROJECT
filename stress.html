Stress

Stress is the body’s natural response to pressure or challenging situations. It often occurs when we feel overwhelmed by academic demands, work responsibilities, or personal problems. While short-term stress can sometimes motivate us to perform better, prolonged or chronic stress can negatively affect both physical and mental health. It may lead to headaches, fatigue, irritability, and difficulty concentrating. Managing stress is important because it helps maintain balance in daily life. Healthy coping strategies such as exercise, relaxation techniques, time management, and talking to supportive friends or family can reduce its impact and promote overall well-being.
