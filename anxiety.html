Anxiety

Anxiety is a feeling of worry or fear about future events. Learning coping strategies and relaxation techniques can help people manage anxiety effectively.

Anxiety is a common mental health condition characterized by excessive worry, fear, or nervousness about future events or situations. It can manifest in various forms, such as generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, or social anxiety disorder. While some level of anxiety is normal and can be helpful in certain situations, excessive or persistent anxiety can interfere with daily activities and relationships. Treatment options for anxiety include therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes.
